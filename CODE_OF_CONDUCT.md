# Code of Conduct

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability,
ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance,
race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We commit to acting in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse,
inclusive, and healthy community: showing empathy and kindness, respecting
differing opinions and experiences, giving and gracefully accepting constructive
feedback, and taking responsibility for our mistakes.

Harassment, trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, publishing others'
private information, and other conduct reasonably considered inappropriate are
not tolerated.

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainer. All
complaints will be reviewed promptly and fairly, with respect for the reporter's
privacy.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1.
